Privacy Policy

Piece of Mind — Last updated March 2026

Piece of Mind does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, no analytics, and no tracking. Your game progress and settings are saved locally on your device only and are never transmitted anywhere.

The app displays ads via Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policy to serve ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
